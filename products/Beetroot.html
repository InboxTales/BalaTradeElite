Beetroot

Beetroot, known for its vibrant red color and sweet, earthy flavor, is a nutritious root vegetable packed with essential vitamins and minerals. Bala TradeElite Pvt. Ltd. ensures that our beetroots are harvested at the peak of freshness, maintaining their quality for export to international markets.

Our beetroots are sourced from the best farms in India, where they are grown under ideal conditions to ensure the highest quality. We offer beetroots with the following specifications:

  • Grade: A-Grade
  • Color: Deep Red
  • Size: Medium to Large
  • Taste: Sweet and earthy
  • Packed: In 10kg, 20kg, 50kg sacks, based on requirements
