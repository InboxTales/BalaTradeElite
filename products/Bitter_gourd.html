Bitter Gourd

Bitter Gourd, also known as bitter melon, is a unique vegetable-fruit that is prized for its medicinal properties and distinctive bitter flavor. Bala TradeElite Pvt. Ltd. exports fresh, high-quality bitter gourds, ensuring they maintain their nutritional value and taste during transit.

Our bitter gourds are sourced from trusted farms in India, where they are cultivated under ideal conditions to produce the best quality. We offer bitter gourds with the following specifications:

  • Grade: A-Grade
  • Color: Green
  • Size: Medium to Large
  • Taste: Distinctively bitter
  • Packed: In 5kg, 10kg, 20kg cartons, based on requirements
